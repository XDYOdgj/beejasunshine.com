Ellis Enterprises Country Floral Swag Pair

Elevate your home with the timeless blend of country charm and sophisticated elegance that this swag pair brings. With its soft, breathable, and durable fabric made from 70% polyester and 30% cotton, it's the perfect addition to your living space, offering both style and function in one.

Soft and Durable Fabric

This swag pair is crafted from a premium blend of polyester and cotton, combining softness with long-lasting durability. Its fabric is gentle on your eyes and tough on wear, making it ideal for daily use in any room.

Lightweight and Easy Installation

Fabric is lightweight, making it effortless to install on standard rods without causing strain on your window hardware. It's designed to allow natural light and airflow while still providing a touch of privacy and a soft, flowing look that enhances your interior style.

Elegant Ruffle Detail

Each swag features delicate ruffles that add a refined, flowing look to your room. These subtle embellishments make the pair a standout accent that complements both rustic and modern interiors effortlessly.

Versatile Fit

With a 2.5-inch header and 1.5-inch rod pocket, these swags are designed to fit standard rods and offer a flexible, convenient solution for your window decor needs.

Easy to Care For

Machine washable for hassle-free maintenance, these swags retain their beauty and structure even after repeated use. They're the perfect choice for those who value convenience without compromising on style or quality.

Why Choose This Swag Pair?

Whether you're looking to add a touch of country charm to your living room or a refined look to your bedroom, these swags are designed to bring warmth and grace to every corner of your home.
