A Custom Metal Urn with a Faith-Inspired Design

This handcrafted urn is a perfect tribute to a loved one who held strong faith or Christian beliefs. Featuring a beautifully engraved sunbeam design, it symbolizes eternal peace and spiritual strength, offering a meaningful way to honor memories with elegance and devotion.

Key Features

  • Unique faith-inspired engraving with personalized text to celebrate your loved one's life and beliefs.
  • Premium aluminum construction with a realistic painted wood grain finish for a warm, natural look.
  • 200 cubic inch capacity is ideal for storing adult cremated remains with comfort and care.
