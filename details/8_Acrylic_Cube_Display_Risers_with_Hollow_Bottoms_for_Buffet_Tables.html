Elevate Your Buffet Table with Acrylic Cube Display Risers

Transform the way you display food, desserts, and decor with our 8-piece acrylic cube display risers. Designed for both elegance and practicality, these risers bring a polished look to any banquet or retail setup. The hollow bottom construction adds visual depth and provides a stable foundation, making it perfect for showcasing cakes, pastries, jewelry, and more in a professional manner.

Each riser features a stackable and nesting design, allowing you to create customizable display tiers for different events or spaces. Whether you're arranging a single item on a table or building a multi-layered setup for a larger display, these risers offer flexible options that help you maximize your presentation. Crafted from high-quality, durable acrylic, they maintain a clear and premium appearance over time, ensuring your products remain beautifully visible and protected.

The set includes 8 mini table signs and 2 dry-erase markers, giving you the ability to personalize each display easily. These risers are ideal for use in a home kitchen, dessert store, or for special occasions like weddings, parties, and art exhibitions.
